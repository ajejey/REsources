Solutions

Clean combustion solutions

REsources works extensively on clean combustion systems to provide clean, efficient and economically viable solutions to our clients. We are incorporating our research and knowledge according to your requirements to conceive, design and manufacture products and services. REsources is a one-stop-solution for your combustion system requirements.

Fruits and vegetables drying solutions

REsources helps you to design and build biomass based systems for fruits, vegetable, herbs and spices drying. Consult us to get in-depth insights on drying systems.

Cloth drying solutions

REsources guides you to design biomass based cloth drying systems for both domestic and industrial uses.

Municipal solid waste solutions

REsources provides solutions for handling MSW. Our green technology is capable of converting combustible part of MSW to value added solid fuel. Solutions are tailor-made to handle the waste at source itself which makes it suitable for auditoriums, office complexes and other institutions.

Clean crematorium solutions

REsources biomass based clean crematorium solutions are unique which allow the traditional wood-based cremation possible without any hustle. The solution ensures minimum usage of wood, smoke/smell free environment along with minimum time for burning.

Products


Vayumithra

HC3-D

Horizontal Continuous Clean Combustion Device


The VAYUMITRA is a continuous combustion device tailored to cater to the needs of domestic to large scale cooking application. Suitable for domestic application, catering units, hotels, restaurants, and canteens.


Features

  • Uses state of the art technology for ensuring clean combustion
  • Variable flame control
  • Fuel Flexibility
  • Easy Ash collection system
  • Uninterrupted operation
  • Smokeless clean cooking experience
  • No soot formation
  • Maximum firewood size of 15-20cm

Agnisakhi

VEBCOD

Vertical Ejector Based Combustion Device


The AGNISAKHI is a clean combustion device uniquely designed for the easy replacement solution for the traditional cook stove systems, in which long logs of wood can be used (limited to window size). Suitable for domestic application, catering units, hotels, restaurants, and canteens


Features

  • Uses state of the art technology for ensuring clean combustion
  • Variable flame control
  • Fuel Flexibility
  • Long wood with width limited to window size can be used
